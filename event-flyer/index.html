Asian Food Fair

Please join us in attending the Asian food fair taking place all day Saturday, March 23 from 11 AM - 9 PM! We will have an assortment of vendors offering food and fare from across the Asian diaspora.

Featured Cuisines

  • Chinese (egg fried rice, dim sum, etc.)
  • Indian (butter chicken, mango lassi, etc.)
  • Korean (bibimbap, bungeo-ppang, etc.)
  • Thai (mango sticky rice, pad thai, etc.)
  • Singaporean (chilli crab, chicken satay, etc.)
  • ...and much more!

Special Activities

  • Origami Workshop
  • K-Pop Random Play Dance
  • Anime Viewing
