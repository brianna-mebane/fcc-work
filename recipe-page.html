A Glass of Water

Today, we are going to learn how to pour ourselves a simple glass of nice cold water!

Ingredients

  • A glass of your choosing
  • A source of cold water (e.g. cold water from the tap, a cold bottle of water, a filtered water pitcher fresh out of the refrigerator, etc.)
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Powdered flavor pack (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place your glass of choice in front of you on a flat surface such as a tabletop, a counter, etc.
  2. If you'd like some ice in your water, make sure to put in your glass now/before you add water.
  3. Fill your glass up with a reasonable amount of water (ideally enough that there's ample space between the top of the glass and the top of however much water you pour into the glass).
  4. If you're adding any flavor powder to your water, pour it in and make sure to mix it in with a spoon or straw. Otherwise, feel free to take a sip from your glass now. Enjoy!
