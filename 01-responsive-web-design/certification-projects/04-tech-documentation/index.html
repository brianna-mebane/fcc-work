Introduction

What is PinkWorld?

PinkWorld is a social media scheduling and analytics platform built for creators and influencers who are serious about growing their audience but don't want to get burnt out in the process.

Whether you're managing one account or juggling five, PinkWorld gives you the tools to plan your content ahead of time, post at exactly the right moment, and understand what's actually working. Less guessing, more growing.

What can you do with PinkWorld?

We're so glad you asked! Using our platform, you can:

  • Schedule posts (Blooms) across Instagram, TikTok, X, YouTube, and Pinterest all from one place
  • Visualize your entire content calendar in The Garden
  • Track reach, engagement, and follower growth in the Garden View dashboard
  • Connect all your social accounts through Portals
  • Get notified the moment something needs your attention with Pings

Who is PinkWorld for?

PinkWorld is designed for independent creators, influencers, and small creator teams who want a focused, intuitive tool, not an enterprise platform with a bajillion features they'll never use.

PinkWorld Jargon
Term Definition
World Your personal PinkWorld workspace & the home base for everything you do on the platform. It holds your connected accounts, your content calendar, your analytics, and your settings. When you sign up, your World is created automatically. If you're on a Creator or Pro plan, you can invite collaborators into your World and work together.
Bloom A scheduled post. Any time you create a piece of content and set it to go live at a specific time, that's a Bloom. Blooms can be scheduled to one Portal or several at once, and they live in The Garden until they're published. After they go live, they remain in your history so you can track their performance.
The Garden Your content calendar where all your Blooms live before they're published. You can view it by month, week, or list, drag and drop Blooms to reschedule them, and see your entire posting plan at a glance. Drafts have their own tab inside The Garden too.
Bloom Times PinkWorld's smart posting suggestions. Based on your audience activity data and historical engagement patterns, PinkWorld recommends specific time windows when your followers are most likely to be online and engaging. You're never required to use them, but they're there if you want to.
Portal A connected social media account. Every platform you link to PinkWorld becomes a Portal. Portals are how PinkWorld knows where to send your Blooms and where to pull your analytics data from. You manage them under Settings → Portals.
Garden View PinkWorld's analytics dashboard. It pulls performance data from all your connected Portals and displays in one unified view your reach, engagement, follower growth, top-performing Blooms, and more. You can filter by date range, platform, or content type, and export reports as a CSV or PDF.
Ping A PinkWorld notification. They show up in your notification center (the bell icon, top right) and sometimes as email alerts depending on your settings. You'll get a Ping when a Bloom fails to post, when you're approaching a plan limit, when a collaborator makes a change to your World, or when PinkWorld has something important to tell you.
Tech Prerequisites
  • A PinkWorld account (free or paid)
  • At least one connected Portal
  • A supported browser: Chrome 110+, Firefox 112+, Safari 16+, or Edge 110+
  • PinkWorld is a web app (no download required!)
Getting Started

Time to get your World up and running!

Getting from zero to your first scheduled Bloom takes less than ten minutes. Here's how:

Step 1 — Create your account.

Head to pinkworld.io/signup and sign up with your email or continue with Google. Once you're in, you'll land in your World — your personal PinkWorld workspace.

🌸 Tip: Your World name defaults to your username, but you can change it anytime under Settings → My World.
Step 2 — Connect your social accounts (Portals).

Before you can schedule anything, you need to connect at least one social account. PinkWorld calls these connections Portals.

To add a Portal:

  1. Go to Settings → Portals.
  2. Click + Add Portal.
  3. Select your platform (Instagram, TikTok, X, YouTube, or Pinterest).
  4. Authorize PinkWorld when prompted by the platform.
  5. Your Portal will appear as connected with a green status indicator.

You can connect up to 5 Portals on the free plan, and unlimited on Creator and Pro plans.

Step 3 — Schedule your first Bloom.

A Bloom is what PinkWorld calls a scheduled post. Here's how to create one:

  1. Click + New Bloom from anywhere in your dashboard.
  2. Select which Portal(s) you want to post to.
  3. Write your caption, upload your media, and add any hashtags or alt text.
  4. Choose a date and time — or click Suggest a Bloom Time to let PinkWorld recommend your best posting window based on your audience data.
  5. Click Plant it! to add it to your Garden.

That's it! Your first Bloom is scheduled.

Scheduling

Every Bloom has the following fields:

Field Description
Portal(s) Which social account(s) to post to.
Media Photo, video, or carousel.
Caption Your post text, including hashtags and mentions.
Alt Text Accessibility description for images.
Scheduled Time The exact date and time your Bloom will go live.
Labels Optional tags to organize your Blooms.

Bloom Times

Click Suggest a Bloom Time when creating a Bloom and PinkWorld will recommend 3–5 optimal posting windows based on your audience activity. Bloom Times get more accurate the longer you use the platform.

Drafts & Queues

Not ready to commit to a time? Save as a draft and come back later. Or add Blooms to the Queue, a pre-set schedule that automatically slots content into your next available Bloom Time.

FAQ

What if my Bloom didn't post at the scheduled time?

Check Settings → Portals for a disconnected account, or look under The Garden → Failed Blooms for a specific error. Also check status.pinkworld.io for any platform outages.

Why does my analytics data look incomplete?

Analytics can take up to 24 hours to fully populate. If data is still missing after that, try reconnecting the affected Portal.

Why can't I upload my video?

PinkWorld supports MP4 and MOV files up to 500MB at 720p or higher. Platform-specific requirements may be stricter, any PinkWorld will flag any issues with specific error messages.

Does PinkWorld post automatically?

Yep, no need for manual approval at post time! If you want to review first, save as a draft and schedule it when you're ready.

Can I collaborate with a team?

Only on Creator and Pro plans. Invite collaborators as an Editor or Viewer under Settings → My World.
