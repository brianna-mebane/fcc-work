Storied Basketball Franchise Wins First NBA Championship

In a nail-biting Game 7 for the ages, the Indiana Pacers defeated the San Antonio 127-119 to nab their first NBA championship in franchise history. With over 75 points contributed from three starters alone and another 50 points provided by their bench, the Blue & Gold topped off a stellar comeback season by hoisting the Larry O'Brien at Frost Bank Arena in San Antonio.
